Privacy Policy

Jodha Bai collects personal information, including your name, email address and phone number, only when you voluntarily submit an enquiry or make a booking through our website or via third party booking platforms. We do not sell, trade or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

Information you provide is used solely to respond to your enquiry, process your booking and improve the guest experience. We may retain correspondence for up to 24 months to assist with future stays. All data is stored securely and access is restricted to retreat management only.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise this right, please contact us at bookings@jodhabairetreat.com. This policy is governed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

Terms & Conditions

All bookings at Jodha Bai are subject to availability and confirmed only upon receipt of full payment or deposit as specified at the time of booking. Rates are quoted in Australian dollars and include GST where applicable.

Cancellations made more than 14 days before arrival will receive a full refund less any third party booking fees. Cancellations within 14 days of arrival are non refundable. We strongly recommend travel insurance. In the event the retreat is unable to honour a confirmed booking due to circumstances beyond our control, guests will receive a full refund or the option to rebook.

Jodha Bai accepts no liability for loss, injury or damage to persons or property during your stay, except where required by law. These terms are governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of NSW.

House Rules

To ensure a comfortable stay for all guests, please observe the following.

  • No smoking inside any building on the property. Designated outdoor areas are provided.
  • Please remove shoes before entering the suites. Slippers are provided for your comfort.
  • Quiet hours are observed between 10 pm and 8 am; please be mindful of neighbouring guests and the natural environment.
  • The retreat accommodates the number of guests specified in your booking. Additional visitors require prior approval from management.
  • Pets are not permitted on the property unless expressly agreed upon at the time of booking.
  • Please treat the property, furnishings and grounds with care. Guests are responsible for any damage caused during their stay.
  • Check in is from 3 pm; check out is by 11 am. Early or late arrangements may be available on request.

We want your stay to feel like home: unhurried, warm and entirely your own. Thank you for helping us keep it that way.
